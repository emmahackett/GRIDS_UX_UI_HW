Tiny Tails Website Redesign

Project Overview

Tiny Tails Rescue Adelaide is a NFP giving neglected animals another chance. Run entirely by volunteers, our goal was to make their lives easier by making their website super easy to navigate, whilst highlighting all the awesome stuff they do. This lead to a focus on the 'Adopt', 'Volunteer' and 'Donate' pages.

// Low-Fi Wireframes

After reviewing competitor sites, we found several functions very useful and knew that Tiny Tails could benefit from them. These features included using hero imagery of the animals to show users the pets available for adoption, enabling users to donate online conveniently and securely, apply to volunteer via an online form and most importantly, enabling users to enquire about available pets so that they could meet them at Tiny Tails' adoption days.

// Style Tile

// Mid-Fi Wireframes

The next step was apply the style tile to the wireframes in preparation for testing. User testing revealed that the supporter logos didn't suit the styles of the website, that some of the text could be better understood as icons or symbols, that some of the form fields required clarity and that some information needed reordering.

// Hi-Fi Wireframes

Taking on board the feedback from the mid-fi wireframe user testing, we adjusted the wireframe to create a hi-fi version and developed the prototype.
